# Sample Voice Notes for Zero-Click CRM Demo

Use these scripts to record audio samples for the demo. You can use:
- Your computer's built-in voice recorder
- Online TTS services (like https://ttsmaker.com/)
- Record yourself reading these scripts

Save as WAV or MP3 files in this directory.

---

She mentioned their budget has been approved, but she needs to present this to their VP of Sales next Monday. Can someone send her a detailed proposal with the implementation timeline? Her email is sarah dot johnson at acmecorp dot com, and her direct line is plus one five five five zero one zero one. Let's make sure we follow up before Monday."

**Expected Extraction:**
- Contact: Sarah Johnson  
- Company: Acme Corporation
- Deal Value: $10,000
- Email: sarah.johnson@acmecorp.com
- Phone: +1-555-0101
- Next Step: Send detailed proposal with timeline

## Recording Tips:

1. **Speak naturally** - Don't sound robotic
2. **Include pauses** - Natural conversation rhythm
3. **Vary your tone** - Show excitement for big deals, casual for quick notes
4. **Pronounce clearly** - Especially emails and phone numbers
5. **Stay under 60 seconds** - Demo efficiency

## Quick Test:

After recording, test by uploading to the CRM and verify all fields extract correctly!
